---

## Suggested Research Questions

- Does the explanation highlight clinically meaningful tumor regions?
- Does the explanation match radiologist or pathologist annotations?
- Is the explanation stable under small input changes?
- Can the explanation improve user trust?
- Does the model rely on artifacts or spurious background patterns?
